Nadia Cakes and Patisserie

Nadia Café & Pâtisserie is located on the entrance floor and is named after the polished stone protruding from the floor in front of the large windows.

During spring, summer and autumn our outdoor terrace is open with afternoon sun and beautiful views of Baggensfjärden. Indoors there are plenty of seats and many tables by the window.

All pastries and bread are made from scratch, with a quality conscious and craftmanship manner by Head of pastry, Annie Hesselstad (who made 2022 years dessert for the Nobel banquet dinner) and the team of pastry chefs and bakers. Here the classic pastry and bread meet modern creations.

The hearty food is presented by Head Chef Patrik Gullmarsvik and his staff. Both patisserie and kitchen find inspiration from the surrounding nature, with the emphasis on season and locally sourced products. Welcome to Nadia Café & Pâtisserie!
